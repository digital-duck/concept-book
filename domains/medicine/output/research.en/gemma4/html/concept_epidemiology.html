🍃 Epidemiology

Epidemiology is defined as the systematic study of the distribution, determinants, and control of health-related states or events within specific populations. Formally, it quantifies the relationship between observed outcomes ($O$) and potential exposure factors ($\mathbf{X}$) by calculating measures like incidence rates ($\lambda$), prevalence, and odds ratios, which must be contextualized within a defined population at risk $P$. The discipline operates on the premise that variations in disease frequencies across space or time reflect underlying differences in biological susceptibility, environmental exposures, or behavioral determinants.
