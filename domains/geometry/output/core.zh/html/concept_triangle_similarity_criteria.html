🍃 Triangle Similarity Criteria

🍃 三角形相似性准则 (Triangular Similarity Criteria)

Imagine you’re an architect designing a bridge. You need to ensure that different sections of the bridge maintain proper proportions and angles – even as the overall size changes. This is where triangle similarity comes in handy. It allows us to determine if two triangles are similar, which means they have the same shape but can be scaled (made larger or smaller) without changing their form. Understanding this concept is crucial not just for architecture but also for fields like navigation and even computer graphics.

定义 (Definition): 两个三角形 $ABC$ 和 $A'B'C'$ 是相似的，如果它们之间存在以下对应关系：

  • $\angle A \cong \angle A'$, $\angle B \cong \angle B'$, 以及 $\angle C \cong \angle C'$.

    • 这意味着这两个三角形具有相同的内角。 此外，对应边也成比例，即 $\frac{AB}{A'B'} = \frac{BC}{B'C'} = \frac{AC}{A'C'}$。

    示例 (Worked Example):

    Consider triangles $ABC$ and $DEF$. We are given that $\angle A = \angle D$, $\angle B = \angle E$, and $\angle C = \angle F$. Let $AB = 3$, $BC = 4$, and $AC = 5$. Also, let $DE = 6$, $EF = 8$, and $DF = 10$.

    Since the corresponding angles are equal, we can conclude that triangles $ABC$ and $DEF$ are similar. We can calculate the ratio of corresponding sides: $\frac{AB}{DE} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}$ $\frac{BC}{EF} = \frac{4}{8} = \frac{1}{2}$ $\frac{AC}{DF} = \frac{5}{10} = \frac{1}{2}$

    Since the ratios are equal, $ABC \sim DEF$.

    为什么它是真的 (Why It's True): 相似性源于角度的保持不变。如果两个三角形共享一个角，并且其余角也相等，那么它们的形状必然相同。 这种保证基于几何中的基本原理，即对应角相等的三角形是相似的。这与平行线的性质相关联，它允许我们推导出相似三角形的关系。

    练习 (Practice Problem):

    Given that $\triangle ABC \sim \triangle XYZ$, with $AB = 5$, $BC = 8$, $CA = 6$ and $XY = 10$, $YZ = 16$, $XZ = 12$. Determine if the triangles are similar. Explain your reasoning.
