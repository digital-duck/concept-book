🍃 Proof Search

A proof search, or automated theorem proving process, seeks to determine if a given conclusion $\psi$ can be logically derived from a set of premises $\Gamma$ within a specified formal axiomatic system $\mathcal{L}$. Formally, it aims to find a finite sequence of formulas $F_1, F_2, \dots, F_k$ such that $F_k = \psi$, and each $F_i$ is either an axiom of $\mathcal{L}$ or is derivable from $\{F_j\}_{j
